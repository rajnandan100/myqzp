Our Mission

To democratize quality education and make competitive exam preparation accessible to every student, regardless of their geographical location or economic background.

  • Provide high-quality study materials for free
  • Create an engaging learning experience
  • Build a supportive learning community

Our Vision

To become the world's leading educational platform that transforms the way students prepare for competitive exams through innovative technology and personalized learning paths.

  • Global reach with local expertise
  • AI-powered personalized learning
  • Continuous innovation in education

Our Journey

Milestones that define our growth

2020 - The Idea

Quiz Portal was conceived during the pandemic when students needed accessible online learning solutions.

2021 - Launch

Launched with 50 quizzes and 100 study materials. Reached 1,000 users in the first month.

2022 - Growth

Expanded to 10,000 active users and introduced premium features with advanced analytics.

2023 - Mobile First

Redesigned the platform with mobile-first approach, resulting in 300% increase in mobile usage.

2024 - Recognition

Won "Best EdTech Platform" award and crossed 50,000 registered users milestone.

2025 - Global Expansion

Expanding internationally and introducing AI-powered personalized learning paths.
